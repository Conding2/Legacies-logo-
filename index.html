Legacies Global

We partner with artisan designers and cultural innovators across Nigeria to produce curated editions and sustainable collaborations — quality, craft, and heritage reimagined for the modern world.

Mission

To build a platform that elevates craftsmanship, ensures ethical growth for creators, and connects local stories to global markets with integrity and care.

Vision

To make Nigerian creativity a first-class global conversation — where design, heritage, and commerce meet to create lasting legacies.

Get Involved

If you're a designer or maker interested in joining Legacies, email legacies@legacies.global — we're building the hub to connect talent and markets.

How we work

We curate small-batch collaborations, emphasize sustainable materials and fair pay, and provide distribution channels to international buyers.
